Deadly Nadder

Class: Sharp

Description: A fast, bird-like dragon with bright-colored scales and sharp spines covering its body.

Abilities:
-Fires magnesium-fueled fireballs
-Can shoot spines from its tail as projectiles
-Extremely agile and precise in flight

Weaknesses:
-Poor endurance in extended battles

Notable Dragon: Stormfly (Astrid's Dragon)
