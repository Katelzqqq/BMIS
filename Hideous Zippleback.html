Hideous Zippleback

Class: Mystery

Description: A unique two-headed dragon that relies on teamwork and strategy. One head releases flammable gas, and the other ignites it.

Abilities:
-Gas-spreading and fire ignition combo attack
-Excellent in deceptive and tactical battles
-Highly intelligent and unpredictable

Weaknesses:
-Can be disoriented if the two heads disagree

Notable Dragon: Barf & Belch (Ruffnut & Tuffnut's dragon)
